FAQ

Answers, at a glance

A few common questions about how Matcha works and what to expect.

Is Matcha free?

Yes. Core features are free. A Plus plan unlocks advanced filters and weekly boosts.

How do you protect my privacy?

We store the minimum viable data, encrypt messages end-to-end, and never sell data to advertisers.

What cities are supported?

We’re live globally. Smart matching automatically adapts to your city’s density.
